Pokémon Legends: Z-A Fan Guide

Choosing your starter Pokémon is one of the most important decisions you’ll make at the beginning of your journey in Pokémon Legends: Z-A. Your starter will be your main partner early on, shaping how you approach battles and your strategy as you learn the game’s mechanics. Each starter Pokémon offers different strengths and weaknesses, as well as playstyles, making the choice more meaningful than just appearance alone. Understanding how your starter fits your preferred way of playing can give you a strong foundation for the challenges ahead.
