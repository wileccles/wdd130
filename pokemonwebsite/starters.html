Pokémon Legends: Z-A Fan Guide

Starter Pokémon: Chikorita
Chikorita is the grass type starter, originating from the Johto Region. According to the pokedex: "A sweet aroma gently wafts from the leaf on its head. It is docile and loves to soak up sun rays."
Starter Pokémon: Tepig
Tepig is the fire type starter, originating from the Unova Region. According to the pokedex: "It can deftly dodge its foe's attacks while shooting fireballs from its nose. It roasts berries before it eats them."
Starter Pokémon: Totodile
Totodile is the water type starter, originating from the Johto Region. According to the pokedex: "It is small but rough and tough. It won't hesitate to take a bite out of anything that moves."
In Pokémon Legends ZA, the classic type triangle of fire, water, and grass is the foundation of strategy in battles. Fire, water, and grass each counter one another in a natural cycle: Fire beats grass, grass beats water, and water beats fire. Knowing this core loops helps you predict matchups and build a more balanced team. Even as new mechanics start to appear, understanding this elemental trio is the simplest way of understanding type advantages. If you're having trouble picking a starter, it might be best to choose one that has a type you think you might not have down the line. Typicall fire types are rarer than water or grass types, with there only being 39 total fire types throughout all of Pokémon.
