[Pokemon informational website Pokemon’s Past A brief introduction to Pokemon Pokemon was created in 1996 by two people, Satoshi Tajiri, and Ken Sugimori. Taijiri was inspired by his childhood of collecting creatures and insects in nature. The name for Pokemon came about by combining the words “pocket” and “monster”. Pokemon are captured inside of pokeballs, which were inspired by gashapon, plastic capsules that usually contain a toy inside, and are dispensed through vending machines. The first generation of pokemon takes place in Kanto, which is named after a real region in Japan, (subsequent regions would have fictional names). The main goal of the game is to capture pokemon, battle them, and work your way up to be the Pokemon champion. Following the creation of the first Pokemon game, it became a hit in Japan, and would make its first debut in America during 1998. The game was so popular that it had an Anime series created for it which was immensely popular, a trading card game, and lots of merchandise. The characters and pokemon became cultural icons, as well as the very memorable music used in the anime, many fans regard the first 151 Pokemon to be the best. Pokemon’s Present 28 years later, and we arrive at the present, Pokemon is still a huge cultural phenomenon, it is the highest grossing media franchise in the world. Instead of 1 generation, there are currently 9, with a tenth on the way, and instead of 151 Pokemon, there are now 1,025. Pokemon’s most recent surge of popularity was in 2016 with the release of Pokemon Go, a mobile game that took the world by storm. Pokemon Go’s popularity has gone down since then, but it still has a very loyal fanbase of people who play it. Time can sometimes bring value, and with Pokemon cards, it did. Some pokemon cards from back in the 1990’s have immense value if they are in good condition, with some special cases going into the hundreds of thousands, to even millions. The main body of the Pokemon franchise are the games, with a new one releasing typically every three years. With each game, new pokemon are added, which allows for more merchandise to be made, and more talk about the pokemon which leads to advertising. Pokemon’s Future Pokemon seems to be continuing to rise in popularity. The tenth generation is the next one to come out, while there is little known about it, people have many guesses as to what country the region will be based on, as well as release dates and such. Fans of Pokemon tend to be especially invested in it, and I think it’s because of the nature of the series and how long it’s been out. Many fans are attached to Pokemon they’ve caught throughout the years, and with the ability to transfer Pokemon to newer games once they’ve released, it hooks a lot of fans to play a game with a Pokemon you might have caught when you were a kid. The range of card game players, to main series game enjoyers, anime watchers, merchandise collectors.. There is a lot of variety to absorb content from this franchise, and it’s likely a reason for its continued success over the years. This Website’s purpose The purpose of this website is to give an overview of how far Pokemon as a franchise has come, to give a refresher of where it started, and where it might be headed. This website is geared towards those who are already fans of pokemon, as well as those who have some knowledge of it, and are curious to know more, or get back into the series. This website is also here so that I can give some of my thoughts on these topics. ]
