Definitely No Bears Here River

Known for its peaceful stretches and no bears whatsoever, this river is perfect for any beginners, or people with ample delicious food in their backpack. Enjoy wide bends, deep pine forests, bears, and plenty of wondeful wildlife to see.

Did Grandma Just Fall off the Raft? River

If you're looking for excitement, and a little less cargo, this river serves up big water and challenging waves. This is our most popular river, and a great alternative to nursing homes, and for our most experienced rafters. What was that splash behind us? Who knows!

Ow I Stubbed My Toe River

Winding through narrow red-rock canyons, this scenic route offers unforgettable views, and unforgettable pain in your foot if you don't watch where you're going. Ideal for overnight trips, relaxed sightseeing floats, or anything where you don't have to walk.
